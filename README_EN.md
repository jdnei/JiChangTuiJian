## What You Should Know Before Choosing a VPN Service
- Compared with direct-connection services, relay-based services are generally more highly recommended. Relay services usually have greater bandwidth, while dedicated-line services mainly offer lower latency because their traffic does not pass directly through the firewall. Dedicated-line services tend to feel smoother and more comfortable in daily use. Ultimately, VPN services compete through financial resources. Speed depends on the provider’s total bandwidth, while stability depends on the quality of the provider’s infrastructure, especially the quality of its upstream routes. Streaming and regional-unlocking performance depends on whether the provider uses good exit-node and unlocking services. Large bandwidth and stability are the most important factors.
- For relay-based services, the protocol is clearly not the most important factor. The stability and bandwidth of the relay servers matter more.
- Backup services are extremely important. When you frequently need unrestricted Internet access, you should not rely on only one provider. It is better to purchase several backup services.
